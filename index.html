What is Forsaken?

is a Roblox asymmetrical survival horror game created and developed by the Forsaken Developer Cast. The game puts Survivors against a Killer in rounds with timers.

Play now!

Here are some of the Survivor characters in the game

Guest 1337

Guest 1337 is a Sentinel Survivor available for purchase for 500 Player PointsPlayer Points. Metaphorically known as the shield of the Survivors.

Chance

Chance is a Sentinel Survivor, available to all and purchasable for 777 Player Points Player Points. He is an extreme gambling addict who gambles with his life to survive.

Elliot

Elliot is a Support Survivor and the first free Support character. As a medic, he can see the aura of injured Survivors and throw pizza slices that provide decent healing.

Dusekkar

Dusekkar is a Support Survivor, costing 600 Player Points. Added as the 9th Survivor in Update 2.1.0 on February 21, 2025, he levitates to silence his footsteps and wields a versatile staff. Spinning and firing the staff can shield Survivors, grant temporary health, or slow the Killer, but these abilities may slow Dusekkar himself.
